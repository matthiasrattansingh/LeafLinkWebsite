About Us

Learn more about who we are and what we do.

Who We Are

At LeafLink, we're passionate about cleaning the environment. Our journey began the two founders who create LeafLink from the ground up. Since then, we've been dedicated to keep the earth clean and make the world a better place. Through our commitment to fighting for green energy, we strive to change the world for the better.

Join Us

LeafLink is more than just a clean renewable website; we're a community that protects each other and strives for a common goal. Whether you're planting trees or using solar panels, we're here to be with you and cultivate a group that follow the same interests. Join us in our journey to save the planet. Together, we can do anything and everything.

About the Website

All images are copyright free – With all designs coming from Canva, the royalty free pictures and images from Pexels, Unsplash, and icons from Flaticon. Videos and other types of animation videos such as GIFS were copyright free and created from Canva or retrieved from Pexels/Unsplash. Our idea behind this website was to simply convey the information present in an effective way while also creating a fun, interactive, and professional site. We wanted the website to, of course, answer the prompt but also go beyond that and create a fun environment that was user friendly. In the end, we strived to create a site that would be enjoyed and resonate with those who interacted with it.
